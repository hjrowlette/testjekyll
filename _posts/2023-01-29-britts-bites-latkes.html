Ingredients:

2 whole russet potatoes, peeled

1 large yellow onion

Shred potatoes and onion n large side of box grater. Place into clean linen dish towel and wring very dry. Can be salted and left to sit and wrung again. Then dump into large bowl,

Into bowl with potato and onion, add two whole eggs, equal parts corn starch and tapioca starch (1/2 c. each), mix. Add salt, pepper and paprika, mix. Mixture should hold shape with formed into patty. If too runny, add additional egg or starch of some kind.

Before frying, make sure your oil is hot enough, can drop in a small piece of vegetable mixture and should fry immediately. If your oil is not hot enough, the latkes will come out oily and soggy.


Fry in vegetable oil, flip when deep golden. Should only need to be flipped once. Once fully cooked, remove to paper towels to dry. Salt immediately while still warm! 

Serve with apple sauce, sour cream, or cream cheese with lox and fresh dill.

Can be reheated in non stick pan over low heat, best are best fresh. If making multiple batches, hold in warm oven (or just eat as they finish).
