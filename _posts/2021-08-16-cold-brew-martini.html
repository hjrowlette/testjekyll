 Changing things around again with a drink recipe, and not just any drink recipe--a cocktail! This recipe is in no way an original, but it's dangerously tasty and I had to share it :)

Ingredients

1 part absolut vodka (or other middle shelf vodka, just nothing cheap)

2 parts kahlúa

3 parts cold brew coffee


Directions

1. Fill a shaker with ice.

2. Add 1 part vodka, 2 parts Kahlúa and 3 parts cold brew coffee.

3. Shake together for 15 - 20 seconds, to make the velvety foam.

4. Strain it into a cocktail glass.
