Matzo Brei
Serves 2

Ingredients
3 sheets matzo (I used salted, GF)
3 eggs
splash of milk
2 tbsp butter
salt

-optional- vanilla extract
Maple syrup for serving

Directions
1. In a wide, shallow dish lightly beat eggs. Add splash of milk, and stir to incorporate. (I added a splash of vanilla extract since I was doing the sweet breakfasty version!)

2. Run matzo sheets briefly under cold water to moisten, then break into roughly one inch pieces. Place all pieces in dish with egg mixture, and stir to incorporate. Let set for 2 minutes to fully soften

3. Heat large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add butter and wait for it to melt and get foamy in the pan.

4. Pour the entire egg and matzo mixture into the skillet, spreading across the whole surface of the skillet. Season lightly with salt.

5. Let cook undisturbed for 3-4 minutes or however long it takes for the bottom to get browned (medium, not light but not black either) and crispy. Flip over (I broke mine in half to make it easier to flip).  Season other side lightly with salt. Let cook until brown and crispy.

6. Plate, and drizzle lightly with maple syrup.


Alternatively, skip the vanilla and maple syrup and top with whatever savory toppings come to mind!
