 I'm on fire! I can't be stopped! More recipes all day! This is literally what I cooked for dinner tonight, I'm suggesting some tweaks below but this is a solid starter recipe. It would be great with chicken or pork on the side or with a salad for a lighter meal! You can also pop and swap any veggies you like in my opinion--spinach, mushrooms, broccoli--just roast em and throw em in! Enjoy!

Ingredients (serves 4)

1/2 lb orzo

2-3 zucchini, halved and sliced

any other vegetables you have laying around (mushrooms, spinach

2-3 large shallots, sliced

1 1/2 cup frozen peas

4-5 cloves garlic

1/2 cup goat cheese

1/2 lemon, juiced

Zest of 1 lemon

olive oil

salt

pepper

thyme (fresh)

 

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 F.

2. Mix olive oil in with the zucchini and onions. season with salt and pepper. Keeping the skin on the garlic cloves, place them around the baking sheet with the zucchini and onions and roast for about 25-30 minutes, mixing them around halfway through.

3. Boil water for the orzo and cook according to package directions (mine was about 11 min). With 5 minutes of cook time left, add the frozen peas to the pot (to save yourself from using another one). Drain and add to a large mixing bowl.

4. Add in the cooked zucchini and onions to the bowl. Squeeze out the roasted garlic cloves from the shell and chop them up fine and add to the mixing bowl.

5. Add in the rest of the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, pepper, thyme, and goat cheese.

6. Mix together and serve!
