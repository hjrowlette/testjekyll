 HURRY! THERE'S NO TIME! just kidding, this recipe is just stupid quick and easy so I'm throwing it up here! This is my new favorite way to eat green beans--they stay gorgeously crisp, go great in a salad, can be finished on the stove in a sauce of your choice, or are just the best standalone snack you can reach for!


Ingredients

green beans. duh.

that's it.


Directions

1. Bring a large pot of water to boil on the stove.

2. GENEROUSLY SALT THE WATER ONCE IT COMES TO A BOIL. I MEAN GENEROUSLY. You actually want it salty like the sea, the green beans are in there so little time that they need the extra salty water.

3. Dump in green beans. Cook 90 seconds (maybe an extra 30 seconds bit more if you're doing a huge amount of beans).

4. Quickly drain, and rinse beans in cold water. Stick beans in a bath of ice water until they have cooled.

5. Add to a salad, leave in the fridge as a snack, or finish in a warm pan with some butter or sauce. Enjoy!
