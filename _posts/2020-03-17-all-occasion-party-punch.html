All Occasion Party Punch (Alcoholic or Non-Alcoholic!)
Serves 12-16

Ingredients
2 L sprite (or champagne)
64 oz (standard size bottle) cranberry juice
52 oz (standard size bottle) orange juice
1 cup vodka (if you desire) (I used equal parts raspberry and lime)
1 cup triple sec (if you desire)

*I would note if you don't use champagne but do use vodka, you can add up to 3 cups of vodka. If you use champagne and vodka I recommend using only the volumes of vodka listed above so that you can, you know, still stand after a glass of it*

Directions
1. Mix together cranberry, orange juice, and vodka (if you desire)

2. Add sprite or champagne just before serving

3. Serve over ice. Garnish with fresh orange slices
