Hey there folks! Back again with a quick and easy (and delicious of course!) sweet potato fries recipe. I've made sweet potato fries a lot of times before, but this is the first time I put thought into it, rather than just throwing it together. Which sound stupid when I say it, but here we are!

Anyways, this recipe is quick and easy, I would just say be sure to take the time to cut the potatoes evenly and small enough so they can cook quickly and get to that delicious crispy and colored state.

Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients
2 lb sweet potato
2 tbsp EVOO
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp black pepper


Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

2.  Peel potatoes, and thinly slice into shoestring size. They should be roughly 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick and 3 inches long. Smaller is better for sure.

3. Toss the sliced potatoes in a bowl with the EVOO. In a smaller bowl mix spices together, then sprinkle on top of the potatoes. Mix well, then spread the potatoes across two baking sheets.

4. Bake until brown and crisp, about 15 minutes. Flip and cook until the other side is crisp, another 10 minutes.

5. Serve hot with a dipping sauce of your choice!
