Throw back to my days working at Buffalo Wild Wings:

Onion Rings:

Cut yellow onion thickly into rings.

Dry Mix:

Plain brown rice flour

Wet mix:

Equal parts white rice flour, brown rice flour, corn starch and tapioca starch. (start with 1/2 c. each)

Add garlic power, onion powder, and paprika (1 Tbsp) each, salt and pepper

Add gluten free beer, mixing constantly until JUST thinned. 

Dredge onion rings in wet batter, then dry batter, then wet batter again. (In hindsight this doesn't seem right to me, interpret with caution). 

Shallow fry until golden. Drain on paper towels.


Southwest Ranch:

1/3 - 1/2 c. mayo (homemade would be best)

1/4 c. sour cream

2 Tbsp. Siracha

1/2 Tbsp. Ketchup

1 Tbsp Adobo sauce (from the can with the chilies)

1 Tbsp ranch seasoning (hidden valley dip packet)

1/2 tsp. cayenne

Salt and pepper

Add everything to bowl, taste and adjust. IT'S SO GOOD.
