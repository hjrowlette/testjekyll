 A frequent "dad"  birthday dinner request, this herby, spicy shrimp with a kick, in a buttery-beer "sauce" is fast, easy, and delicious.  Serve as an appetizer, or for dinner over rice OR with crusty bread to soak up the tasty sauce!

Ingredients

2 lbs shrimp shelled and deveined

4 Tbsp butter

2 tsp minced garlic

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp dried basil

1 tsp dried thyme

1 tsp cayenne pepper

1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

1/2 tsp salt (to taste)

1/2 tsp black pepper (to taste)

1/2 cup beer


Directions

Saute garlic and herbs in butter until garlic is slightly browned.  Add shrimp, stirring constantly, until shrimp are pink and cooked.  Pour in beer, simmer one minute and your are ready to serve!  That is a wrap folks!
