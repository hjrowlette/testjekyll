 This is the perfect appetizer or side dish for a meal! Super quick to pull together, and you don't need a bunch of ingredients. Enjoy!


15-minute Miso Soup with Greens and Tofu


Ingredients (4 servings)

8 cups vegetable broth or dashi (I used dashi stock powder, you could make your own dashi though!)

2 sheets nori, cut into large squares 

6-8 tbsp white or yellow miso

1 cup chopped chard or sturdy green (I used kale)

1 cup chopped scallion

1/2 cup firm tofu (optional)

sliced mushrooms (optional)


Directions

1. Place broth in medium saucepan and bring to a low simmer

2. Place miso in a small bowl, add a little hot water and whisk until smooth (to prevent clumping when adding to soup later)

3. Add chard, scallions, mushrooms and tofu (if using) to the broth and cook for 5 minutes. Add nori and stir.

4. Remove from heat, add miso and stir.

5.Taste and add more miso or sea salt if desired. Serve warm, best fresh.
