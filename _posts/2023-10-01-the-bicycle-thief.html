 This is an interesting one! I saw the bicycle thief on instagram and the flavor combination peaked my interest, and I'm really glad I gave it a shot! Campari is usually thought of for negronis or a spritz, but this is a really tasty, spicy, and complex cocktail that is bright pink, making it super fun! 

Then funnily enough a few months later I came across the italian greyhound and realized its almost identical to bicycle thief, just uses aperol instead of campari and lime juice instead of lemon and skip the club soda! It ends up much lighter and a bit sweeter on the palate, just as enjoyable as it's sister bicycle thief!


The Bicycle Thief

Ingredients

½ oz fresh lemon juice

1 ½ oz fresh grapefruit juice

½ oz simple syrup

1 oz Campari

1 oz gin

Club soda 

Orange slice garnish (optional)

 

Directions

1. Combine lemon juice, grapefruit juice, simple syrup, Campari, and gin in cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake until shaker is frosted.

2. Fill glass halfway with ice, and strain cocktail into the glass. Top with club soda. Garnish with orange wheel or citrus peel as desired.


The Italian Greyhound

Ingredients

½ oz fresh lime juice

1 ½ oz fresh grapefruit juice

½ oz simple syrup

1 oz Aperol

1 1/2 oz gin


Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake until shaker is frosted. Strain and serve!
