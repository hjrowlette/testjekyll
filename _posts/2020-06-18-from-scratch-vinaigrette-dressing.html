Number Two: an easy from scratch salad dressing that you throw together, stick in a jar, and pull out when you need it!


I will say, this dressing does separate after a few days in the fridge, so I would store it in a microwave safe container; if you pop it in microwave and warm it up, give it a shake, and it'll come right back together!


The New Standard Vinaigrette
Ingredients
1 1/2 cups EVOO
3/4 cup any combination of vinegar (red wine, white wine, apple cider)
1 tbsp dijon mustard
1 tbsp honey
1-2 shallots, thinly sliced
kosher salt

Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a resealable jar or container; shake vigorously until emulsified, about 30 seconds. season with salt

2. Store in the fridge for a month
