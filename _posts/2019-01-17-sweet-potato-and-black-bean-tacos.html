Here is another great recipe that pairs well with arepas, but also goes great with premade corn tortillas if you don't have the time! Its a great non-meat based option that is so so tasty :)



http://brokeassgourmet.com/articles/sweet-potato-and-black-bean-tacos


Ingredients
1 large (about 7" long) red garnet yam (sweet potato), scrubbed and diced into 3/4" cubes
1/2 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp Ancho chili powder
salt and pepper to taste
1 15-oz. can black beans, rinsed
1 avocado, peeled, pitted, and sliced
1 handful fresh cilantro, chopped
lime wedges
optional (but so so good)--goat cheese or queso fresco


Directions
1. Heat the olive oil in a large, heavy bottomed frying pan over medium heat. Add the diced yam and stir well to distribute the oil.  Let cook, stirring occasionally, for about 8 minutes, until the yams are cooked through (watch them carefully to make sure they don't burn).  

2. Once the sweet potatoes are nearly cooked through, add onion and garlic and allow to soften, 3-5 minutes. 

3. Add rinsed black beans, cumin, and chili powder into mixture and cook another 2-3 minutes.

4. Serve on warmed arepas or corn tortillas with avocado, cilantro, lime wedges, and cheese
