White Bean Hummus

1 can cannelini beans (or other white beans)

1 jalapeno (how much you use is based on how spicy your jalapeno is, or how spicy you want your hummus to be)

1 lemon or lemon juice

3 tbsp tahini

kosher salt

2 cloves garlic

3 tbsp EVOO

chopped herbs as desired

dried spices as desired


This is a pretty loose recipe; I basically throw everything in the blender, mix it, and then see how it tastes and how the texture is. You can always adjust this accordingly; whether you need more jalapeno, lemon, EVOO, tahini, or salt.

My favorite thing is to do a basic hummus but add toppers like fresh herbs, zaatar or another spice mix along with some nice EVOO drizzle. 

If you have roasted garlic or red peppers around, you could absolutely throw those in the blender too; if you want to top with oil-packed sun dried tomatoes? Go for it! Don't have cannelini beans, try black beans! This is definitely more of a guide than a set recipe so take it and run with it :)
