**This is originally a vegan recipe, just use a vegan yogurt and vegan butter and I'm sure it'll be just as good! I just didn't have either**

Buffalo Chickpea Sandwich (with ranch slaw)

(enough for 3 sandwiches)

Ingredients

Chickpea Mixture

1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed

¼ cup red onion, finely diced

½ bell pepper, finely diced

1 clove garlic, grated

½ tsp smoked paprika

½ tsp ground coriander

2 tbsp Greek yogurt

2 tbsp tahini

Juice from ½ lemon

2-3 tbsp buffalo sauce (store bought or homemade recipe below)

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Ranch slaw

1 ½ cups cabbage, shredded

2 medium celery stalks, thinly sliced

Juice from ½ lemon

2 tbsp tahini

 2 tbsp Greek yogurt

1 tbsp nutritional yeast

½ tsp garlic powder

1 tsp onion powder

½ tsp dry dill

½ tsp dry parsley

2-3 tbsp water

 

Buffalo sauce

2 1/2 tbsp hot sauce of choice (I used cholula)

2 tbsp butter

1 tbsp white vinegar

Dash of Worcestershire sauce

Dash of cayenne

Dash of garlic powder

 

Directions

For Buffalo Sauce

1.      In a small saucepan, combine all ingredients and place over medium heat.

2.      Cook until bubbling around the edges, should take only a few minutes.

 

For Chickpeas

3.      In one bowl, add chickpeas and mash with a fork to desired consistency, I leave a few pieces/chunks whole

4.      Add the onion, pepper, spices, garlic, yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, buffalo sauce, salt and pepper, and fold together. If dry, add a bit of water to loosen. Set aside


For Slaw

5.      Combine tahini, yogurt, lemon juice, spices and herbs along with salt and pepper. Add water and whisk.

6.      If you are serving all sandwiches immediately, mix dressing with cabbage and celery. If prepping ahead, store dressing and slaw separately.


Assemble Sandwich

7.      Toast bread; spread with chickpea mixture and then top with slaw. Enjoy!
