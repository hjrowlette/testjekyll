Ingredients
1 medium spaghetti squash, peeled and cubed into bite sized pieces
1 cup chopped onion
3 cups peeled and diced sweet potato (1-2 medium potatoes in bite sized pieces)
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp EVOO
1 (15 oz) can diced tomato
3 (15 oz) cans of black beans (OR COUGH COUGH YOUR POT OF BEANS)
1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp hot chili powder
2 1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
4 cup veg broth (I decided to buy vegetable broth but if you have chicken on hand it'll be fine)
1 small can diced green chilis
1/2 tsp liquid smoke
1/2 tsp Worcestershire

Optional toppings: sliced jalapeno, radish, corn chips, cilantro, greek yogurt, lime wedges)

Directions
1. Heat large pot or dutch oven over medium heat and add EVOO, onion, and a pinch of salt. Cook onions for a few minutes, then add garlic and cook another few minutes.

2. Add squash and sweet potatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Add remaining ingredients, except broth and stir until everything is combined.  Then add broth, bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and cook until sweet potatoes are fork tender, about 40-50 minutes (longer if you like).

 4. Top however you like!
