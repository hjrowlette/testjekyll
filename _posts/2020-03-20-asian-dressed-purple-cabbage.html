Asian-Dressed Purple Cabbage Slaw
Ingredients
4 tbsp soy sauce
4 tbsp oil (I used half sesame, half EVOO)
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp lemon juice
2 tbsp rice vinegar
1 clove garlic, grated
1 2-inch piece of ginger, grated

1 cup shredded carrot
2 scallions, thinly sliced
2-4 cups finely sliced purple cabbage

Directions
1. Stir together all ingredients but the carrot, scallion, and purple cabbage in a small bowl.

2. Mix carrot, scallion, and purple cabbage in a large bowl. Pour dressing on top, and stir to combine.

This tastes best after it marinates for a few hours, but doesnt do so well after a few days in the fridge. Could be used as a side, put on top of fish tacos or burgers, or a number of other things!
