This rich creamy dressing was a love at first bite random find on the internet  

written by Jenn Segal from once upon a chef, adapted from Gourmet Magazine


Ingredients

2 small cloves of garlic

1 tsp anchovy paste

Juice of one lemon (2 Tbsp)

1 tsp dijon mustard

1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

1 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan-reggiano

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp ground black pepper


Instructions

In a medium bowl, whisk together the garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, dijon, and Worcestershire sauce.  Add the mayonnaise, parmesan, salt and pepper and whisk until combined.  Taste and adjust to your liking.  Makes about 1 1/2 cups and lasts about a week in the fridge.
