This is a winner winner tofu dinner recipe! It seems like a lot but it came together really easily, and it can be used in so many different ways. Over rice, in a lettuce wrap (check the next blog post!), or even in a sandwich. The recipe is vegan and still packed with flavor, and its a nice change to add to the tofu rotation. enjoy!

Sesame Crusted Tofu

Ingredients

Tofu
1, 16 oz block extra firm tofu, pressed for 15 minutes and drained
2 tbsp tamari
Juice of 1 lime

Breading
heaping 1/3 cup black and white sesame seeds
heaping 1/3 cup panko bread crumbs
heaping 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
heaping 1 tsp onion powder
heaping 1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp kosher salt
heaping 1/4 tsp white pepper
Zest of 1 lime

Batter
1/3 cup all-purpose or wheat flour
2 tbsp ground flaxseed
1/2 cup unsweetened almond or soy milk (whatever milk you have around, could also use water)
1 tbsp tamari

1-2 tbsp neutral oil

Directions

1. Slice the block of tofu equal 1/4 inch slabs (you can approximate, should end up with 6-10 slices). Salt tofu slices and wrap in paper towels or dish towel, and press for 15-30 minutes.

2. After theyâ€™ve been pressed, place the slabs of tofu in a shallow dish then drizzle the tamari and lime juice overtop. Turn and flip the tofu to make sure it is evenly coated then marinade for 30ish minutes, flipping the slices halfway.

3. Preheat the oven to 425F and drizzle the oil onto a baking tray. To one shallow dish add all the breading ingredients and whisk to combine. To a separate shallow dish, add the flour and flaxseed then whisk together to combine. Pour the milk and tamari into the flour and whisk together until smooth.



4. Take the tofu and dip it into the batter to coat on all sides. Shake off excess batter then transfer to the dry breading, making sure it is coated in the breading on all sides. Press breading into tofu to ensure a thick, even coating.

5. Transfer the breaded tofu to the baking tray and press to coat side with oil, then flip onto the opposite side so both sides are properly coated with oil. Place the tofu in the oven to bake for 10-15 minutes, flip and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until golden.
