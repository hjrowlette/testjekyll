 This is an effort to save Pascale sending my mother the same recipe for yet another year in a row! We made this for easter this year and it was fabulous, came together so easily and made enough for two standard 8-9 inch pans or 3 6-inch pans and a few cupcakes to boot. Classic carrot cake and cream cheese frosting--what can go wrong?!

Classic Carrot Cake and Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients

CAKE 

2 cups sugar

1 1/2 cooking oil

4 eggs

2 cups flour

2 tsp baking soda

1 tsp salt

3 tsp cinnamon

3 cups carrots, freshly grated

1 cup chopped nuts (I recommend 50-50 walnut and pecan) 

1 cup raisins (I recommend golden)


FROSTING

16 oz cream cheese, room temperature

1/2 cup (1 stick) butter

4 cups confectioner's sugar, sifted

2 tsp vanilla


Directions

Cake
1. Grease and flour 2 9" cake pans. Preheat oven to 300 degrees

2. Beat sugar and oil together. Add eggs and mix well

3. In a separate bowl combine dry ingredients. Add to sugar/oil mixutre

4. Fold in carrots, nuts, and raisins

5. Split batter between pans. Bake 50-60 minutes at 300

6. Let cool and frost


Frosting

7. Combine cream cheese and butter in a bowl. Beat in confectioner's sugar and vanilla until it is smooth and spreadable.
