It's been too long but we coming back strong! This is a fabulous recipe that comes together really quickly and is unbelievably tasty. It comes together relatively quickly, especially if you do your eggs ahead of time! 

You can make this gluten free if you use gluten free ramen noodles (or substitute spaghetti noodles), and vegetarian if you swap in additional mushrooms instead of ground meat. Go crazy with it!

Ingredients:

1 lb of Ground Pork or Chicken

1 cup of sliced Mushroom (shitake or baby bella)

1.5 tbsp of minced Garlic (5-6 cloves)

1 tbsp of grated Ginger

1.5 tbsp of La DoubanJiang, Sambal Oelek, or Gochujang

2 tbsp Soy Sauce

2 tbsp of Miso Paste

4 cup Dashi Stock

4 cup Soy Milk unsweetened (could use regular milk)

Ramen Noodles

 

Toppings:

Ramen Eggs

Green Onion

Corn


Instructions:

1. Prep your ramen eggs and cook noodles; set both aside. Prep dashi stock (I just used a kettle and dashi stock powder which made this very quick).

2. In a pot, add in oil and turn the heat to medium-high. Once the pot is hot, saute ground pork or chicken for 2-3 minutes.

3. Add in sliced mushroom, minced garlic, grated ginger, soy sauce, la doubanjiang, and saute together for 2-3 minutes.

4. Remove half of the pork and mushroom to use a topping later.

5. Pour in unsweetened soy milk, dashi stock, and miso paste (dilute miso in the dashi or milk). And let is simmer for another 4-5 minutes.

6. Assemble the ramen by adding noodle to individual bowls, pour on the stock, and top with any topping of your choice.

Easy Ramen Eggs:

Bring a covered pot of water with a splash of vinegar to boil.

Lower heat to medium-high (should be bubbling but not rolling boil). Gently lower eggs into water (4-5 eggs)

Immediately cover and cook 6 minutes.

Remove eggs and place under cold running water or in an ice bath

You can peel and use at this point, or marinade the eggs 2-24 hours in a mixture of mirin and soy sauce 3:1 ratio. (3/4 cup to 1/4 cup)
