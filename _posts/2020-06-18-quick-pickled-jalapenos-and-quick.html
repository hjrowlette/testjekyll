These are super easy to prep and forget. Just make em, pop em in the fridge, and use em for sandwiches, salads, grain bowls, or anything else that comes to mind!







Quick Pickled Jalapenos

Ingredients
1 cup white vinegar
1 cup water
2 cloves garlic, smashed
2 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp salt
7-8 jalapeno peppers, thinly sliced

Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients but jalapenos in a medium pot and bring to a boil. Add jalapeno slices, stir, and remove from heat.

2. Let sit for at least 8 minutes then transfer jalapenos into a jar; cover with brining liquid from the pot to fill up the jar.

3. Store in fridge for up to two months.






Quick Pickled Red Onion

Ingredients
1 large red onion, peeled and very thinly sliced
3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tsp fine sea salt
1-2 tbsp sweetener (honey, maple syrup, brown sugar, etc)

Instructions
1. In a small saucepan stir together vinegar, salt, and desired sweetener. Cook over medium-high heat until mixture reaches a simmer.

2. Place thinly sliced onions in a jar. Pour hot vinegar over the onions--stir until combined. Let marinate for 30 minutes.

3. Store in fridge for up to two weeks.
