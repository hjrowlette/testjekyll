This isn't so much of a recipe post as an idea post of how to combine some of the recipes I've recently posted as well as some old favorites. I had a week that I really wanted to eat a lot of veggies and 
healthy grains, and wanted an easy prep for everything. This is what I came up with, and while it was a busy few hours of chopping, boiling, sauteing, and mixing, the results were incredible. Enjoy!

These are all just ideas, take them as inspiration and run with them!









Grains, Greens, and whatever else you have bowls
-Roasted cauliflower (follow cauliflower taco baking instructions)
-cooked quinoa (follow package instructions, use chicken broth)
-cooked lentils (follow lentil salad cooking instructions)
-sauteed greens (follow greens and beans cooking instructions)
-vinaigrette (see post)
-pickled onions (see post)
-pickled jalapenos (see post)
-white bean hummus (see post)
-fresh veggies (I recommend chopped cukes, cherry tomatos, and whole romaine lettuce leaves)
-fresh herbs (cilantro and parsley are great)
-others: pre baked tofu, feta cheese

other ideas: roasted squash, poached eggs, falafel
