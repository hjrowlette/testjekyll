Ingredients
Sauce
1 green chili, such as a serrano, finely grated
1 garlic clove, finely grated
1/4 c. cashew or almond butter
3 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
salt

Assembly
3 garlic cloves, finely grated
1/4 c. neutral oil
2 tsp. cumin
2 tsp. smoke paprika
2 medium heads of cauliflower, cut into 1"-2" florets
Salt
Corn Tortillas
1small white onion, thinly sliced
Sliced avocado, slices radishes, cilantro leaves and lime wedges (for serving)

Directions:

Using a fork, mix chile, garlic, cashew butter, lime juice and 3 Tbsp. water in a small bowl to combine; season with salt and set aside.

Place a rack in lowest position; preheat oven to 450. Stir garlic, oil, cumin, and paprika in a small bowl and combine. Arrange cauliflower on a rimmed baking sheet and pour spiced oil over. Season with salt and toss to evenly coat cauliflower. Roast without moving until dark brown and crisp on the bottom, 15-20 minutes. Removed from oven and turn florets over. Continue to roast until second side is well sear, 15-20 more minutes.

Working in batches heat corn tortillas in large skillet until warmed through.

Smash avocado on tortillas and top with cauliflower, onion, radishes and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing.

I also picked some red onion in apple cider vinegar and it was super yummy on top!
