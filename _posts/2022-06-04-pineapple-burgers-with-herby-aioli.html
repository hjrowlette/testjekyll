Super summery and tasty burgers inbound! I haven't made burgers for myself before since I don't really have a grill but I did these in a cast iron and I've been missing out--cast iron burgers inbound from now on! Obviously if pineapple isn't your thing you can skip it and add any other toppings, but the sauce and everything else is a pretty perfect pairing!

Ingredients (4 servings)

1 lb (450 g) ground beef

4 slices of fresh pineapple, 1 cm thick, core cut out

4 burger buns

Salt and pepper

1 shallot, finely sliced

Lettuce

Olive oil

 

Sauce

3 dollops or 1/3 cup mayo

2 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 jalape√±o, finely chopped

1 pinch kosher salt

 

Directions

1. Prepare the grill, or heat a cast iron skillet over the stove, on medium heat.

2. Divide the meat into 4 balls and flatten them. Season each side with salt and pepper.

3. Place the patties and pineapple on the grill or in the cast iron skillet. Cook the pineapple on each side, until slightly charred. Cook the patties to preference. At the end, toast the buns, for 2-3 minutes.

4. For the sauce, combine all ingredients in a bowl.

5. To assemble, spread a dollop of sauce on the bun. Top with lettuce, season the lettuce with salt and olive oil, place the beef patty on top, pineapple and shallot. Sandwich with remaining half bun. Serve right away.
