 Hmmm, where to start.  This recipe has been through an assortment of variations over the years, so many in fact, we have had a difficult time keeping track of the "best" ingredients and proportions.  Hopefully, this is indeed the correct one, if not... someone let me know or make the adjustments as needed lol!  Its a comforting, creamy, delicious recipe, and we all know... the meat waits on risotto because risotto waits on nothing !

Ingredients

1 onion, diced

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 Tbsp olive oil

1 Tbsp butter

1/2 cup white wine

4 cups warm chicken stock

1/3 cup freshly grated parmesan

1 1/2 cups risotto

sea salt and black pepper to taste and an additions Tbsp of butter to finish if desired

Directions

In a large saucepan, gently saute the onion and garlic in the olive oil and butter over low heat for 5 minutes. Stir in the risotto and cook for one minute or until liquid is absorbed.  Add wine and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently for 3 minutes. Stir in stock one cup at a time, allowing liquid to be absorbed each time.  Should take 15-18 minutes.  Finally, stir in parmesan, butter, salt and pepper as desired and serve immediately.
