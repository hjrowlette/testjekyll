This is a quick and easy salad to riff however you like it! I'll throw some instructions for the green beans on a separate recipe because it's my new favorite way to eat green beans, and they are so good in this salad. The biggest thing is to chop everything (including your lettuce of choice) evenly sized, preferably on the small side so that it all mixes well and you get a little of everything in every bite. Super punchy, herby, just all around great!

Ingredients

mix (all cut to the size of your beans)

two handfuls each of chopped radicchio, arugula and iceberg or romaine (or whatever mix you have on hand!)

a handful of diced vegetables (I love a mix of cucumber and celery, could do bell pepper)

a hearty pinch of finely chopped parsley leaves

Some diced, blanched and shocked green vegetables, like green beans or potatoes

a can of (or 1 3/4 cups home-cooked) beans, drained -- red kidney, cannellini, garbanzo, pinto -- any bean you like!

a petite handful of diced salami (optional), or even mortadella

a petite handful of diced white cheddar (mozzarella would also work)

 

dressing

1 garlic clove, grated

2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

a pinch of oregano

a dab of mustard (I like dijon)

a wee squirt of honey

1/4 cup EVOO

salt to taste

freshly ground black pepper

 

Directions

1. Combine the salad ingredients in a bowl! (make sure your blanched green beans and canned beans aren't too wet, otherwise you get a soggy salad, and no one wants a soggy salad)

2. Combine the dressing ingredients in another bowl or container, then stir it well and season it to taste with salt.

3. Toss as much dressing as you want with as much salad as you want. Place in a bowl (ideally a big goofy cereal bowl)

4. Eat with a soup spoon for a zippy good time!
