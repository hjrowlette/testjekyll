Sweet Potatoes With Tahini Butter Chickpeas

Ingredients

4 tbsp Butter

3 Tbsp Tahini

1 ½ tsp Soy Sauce

1 Tsp Maple Syrup

1 Tbsp Lemon or Lime Juice

3-4 Sweet Potatoes

2 cans of chickpeas

1.5 tsp smoked paprika

½ tsp paprika

¼ tsp onion powder

¼ tsp garlic powder

1-2 tbsp cornstarch

1 tsp salt

1 tsp pepper

 

150g (roughly ½ bag) Spinach (Or Kale)

Sesame Seeds

Crispy Chilli Oil

2 Spring Onions

 

Directions

1. Melt the butter. Add the tahini, soy sauce, maple syrup, lime juice, and 1 tbsp of warm water to a bowl and mix well. It might curdle but keep mixing and add a little more water if you need to thin it down as each tahini brand is slightly different.

2. Prick the potatoes all over with a fork and rub with a little olive oil, salt and pepper. Cook potatoes at 400F for 50 minutes or until tender.

3. Meanwhile, cook the chickpeas. Drain the chickpeas, dry with a paper towel and place them in a bowl. Mix spices in a smaller bowl, then toss with chickpeas to coat. Add chickpeas to a large frying pan with a drizzle of olive oil. Fry for about 8 mins on medium heat until they start to brown and pop. Turn off the heat, add the spinach, and stir for another few minutes until the spinach has wilted.

4. When your potatoes have cooked and are very tender, place them on a plate and slice each one down the middle. Add a little butter into the middle of each one at this point, if you want to. Use a fork to mash the potatoes flat and season them with salt and pepper.

5. Scatter chickpeas over the potatoes then drizzle plenty of tahini butter over the top. Finish with some crispy chilli oil, sesame seeds, and finely sliced spring onion.
