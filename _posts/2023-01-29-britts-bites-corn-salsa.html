 Mango & Corn Salsa:

2 grilled ears of corn (or broiled), cut off the cob

1 minced jalapeno (can be grilled as well, make sure to wipe off charred skin)

1/3 c. red onion minced (add more to taste)

1/4 c. chopped cilantro

1/3 c. crumbled queso fresco (or other dry cheese like feta)

1/2 of a mango, diced

salt, pepper, oil


Add everything to bowl, and taste and adjust. 

Great on tacos or as a dip with warm tortilla chips.
