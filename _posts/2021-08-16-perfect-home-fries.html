 I will be the first to admit I am absolutely terrible at making potatoes. It doesn't really matter how-mashed, roasted, fried- me and potatoes are just not compatible. UNTIL I FOUND THIS RECIPE. What a game changer; I've made these twice and they are fool proof. Maybe I just needed a decent recipe to follow instead of my usual habit of winging it, but I won't complain about having something that gives me perfect oven roasted potatoes! My only notes are don't overcrowd your potatoes, use plenty of oil, and switch out the seasoning for whatever your needs may be. Enjoy!

Home Fries

Ingredients

3 Medium Idaho potatoes, (or 2 large potatoes)

1 1/2 Tablespoons Olive Oil

1 ¼ Teaspoon Seasoned salt

¾ Teaspoon Dried rosemary

1 tsp paprika

1 tsp garlic powder


Directions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F.

2. Dice potatoes into ¾ to 1-inch cubes.

3. In mixing bowl, toss potatoes with olive oil, seasoned salt, and dried rosemary.

4. Bake at 425 degrees Fahrenheit for 35 to 45 minutes, flipping potatoes occasionally.

5. Serve hot or refrigerate in sealed container for up to 2 days.
