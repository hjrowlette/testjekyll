Lacey Almond Cookies
Ingredients
3/4 cup almond flour
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tbsp gluten free flour
1 tbsp cream
1 tbsp whole milk

Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350F

2. Combine all ingredients in saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly until butter melts

3. Spoon teaspoon-fulls onto parchment-lined cookie sheet and lightly spread out the dollops

4. Bake 7-9 minutes
