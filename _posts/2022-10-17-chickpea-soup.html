This would be very easy to make vegetarian or vegan; the original recipe I found was vegan but I added the bacon and parmesan because hey, I had it around and figured it wouldn't hurt!

Chickpea Soup

Ingredients

2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed

3-4 cups veg broth

2 tbsp miso

1 tbsp EVOO

1 small leek or small yellow onion

A few tbsp chopped bacon (optional)

5 garlic cloves, minced

1 large potato, diced into ½ inch pieces

½ tsp crushed red pepper

1 tsp paprika

a few tbsp Vermouth or white wine

2 small springs rosemary or 1 tsp dried rosemary

2 sprigs fresh thyme or ½ tsp dried thyme

1-2 cups chopped broccoli florets

Lemon

Parmesan

 

Directions

1.      Blend 1 can of chickpeas with 1 cup veg broth and miso

2.      Heat oil in saucepan over medium. Cook leek/onion until translucent (4 min). Add bacon and cook another 2 minutes. Add garlic, potato, red pepper, and paprika. Cook 2 minutes, then deglaze with a bit of white wine or vermouth.

3.      Add remaining chickpeas, veg broth, and pureed chickpeas. Bring to a simmer and add rosemary and thyme. Cover and simmer on low for 10 minutes or until potato is fork tender.

4.      Remove rosemary and thyme, and add in broccoli florets. Cook until florets are tender, another 2-3 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon to taste. Grate in parmesan as desired, ½ cup to 1 cup.

5.      Top with a drizzle of chili oil and more parmesan
