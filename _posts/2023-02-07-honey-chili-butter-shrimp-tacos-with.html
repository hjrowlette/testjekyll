Honey-Chili Butter Shrimp Tacos with Jalapeño-Basil Avocado Cream

Ingredients

1 pound small shrimp, peeled & deveined

Honey-Chili Butter:
4 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
1 tbsp honey
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp chipotle chili powder
1 tsp lime zest
1/2 tbsp olive oil

Jalapeño-Basil Avocado Cream:
1 large ripe avocado
6 tbsp sour cream or full fat Greek yogurt
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
Juice of 1 lime
2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1 garlic clove, crushed
1/2 small jalapeño, chopped and seeds removed

Tacos:
Tortillas of choice (warmed)
Shredded cabbage
Honey
Chopped Scallions

-pickled red onions or jalapenos would also be great on top-

 

Directions

1. Make the butter. Add the butter, paprika, chili powder, salt, lime zest, honey and oil to a small bowl. Mix until well combined. Set aside.

2. Make the cream. Add the avocado, sour cream, basil, cilantro, jalapeño, cumin, salt, garlic and lime juice to a food processor and process until smooth. Set aside.

3. Add the shrimp to a bowl, season liberally with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil. Toss to coat.

4. Heat a skillet over medium high heat, and add the shrimp. Sauté for about 1 1/2-2 minutes per side, then add 1-2 tablespoons of the butter and turn off the heat. Toss the shrimp so they are coated in the butter.

5. Spread the cream on the tortillas, then some cabbage, a spoonful of shrimp and fold the tacos over to close. Drizzle with honey and add chopped scallions.
