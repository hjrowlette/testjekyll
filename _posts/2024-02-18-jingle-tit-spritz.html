 warning, this cocktail is delicious and dangerous in equal measure. It's a perfect winter citrus holiday cocktail that you can scale up and mix ahead of time (minus ice) in a pitcher and let everyone pour them and top with bubbles, It has three sources of alcohol so its much more boozy than it tastes, and it does lean sweet. If you like your cocktails a bit more acidic I would increase your lemon juice just a tad. Enjoy!


Jingle T*ts Spritz

Ingredients

0.75oz Gin

0.5oz Aperol

0.5oz Simple Syrup

0.5oz Lemon

1oz Mandarin Orange Juice

Finish with bubbles (prosecco, champagne, cava, or even soda water)

 

Directions

1. Mix gin, Aperol, simple syrup, lemon juice, and mandarin orange juice in a cocktail shaker. Fill 2/3 with ice and shake until frosted

2. Strain into a glass and top with bubbles of your choice
