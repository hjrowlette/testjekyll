Well it sums it up in the name! This is a ridiculously quick an easy breakfast or dessert pastry that gives off major toaster strudel vibes; I will caveat that by saying I've only had toaster strudel like twice in my life so I'm making some assumptions about what it tastes like. You'll have to forgive the photos on this one, its definitely not the most appetizing looking, but it sure as heck tastes good! It's flaky, crumbly, jammy, and tart. All the best things for a quick and easy dish to throw together in an hour!



Prasselkuchen

Ingredients

Streusel

150g flour

75g sugar

100g butter (1 stick), room temp

1 tsp vanilla

 

Kuchen

1 sheet puff pastry, thawed

6oz raspberry jam (or other jam of your choice!)

 

Glaze

80g powdered sugar

1-2 tbsp fresh lemon juice (about 1 lemons worth)


Optional - zest lemon and rub into a tablespoon or two of demerara sugar

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Follow package instructions to thaw and unfold puff pastry.

2. Combine all streusel ingredients in a bowl and mix (pastry cutter or fork is smart) until there are no more dry bits


3. Line baking sheet with parchment paper

4. On floured surface, roll puff pastry a few inches in one direction. Lay pastry onto parchment paper

5. Spread jam (reserving a few tbsp) over pastry in a thin even layer.

6. Top jam with the crumbled streusel and then dollop remaining jam over the streusel. If you want, sprinkle lemon zest and demerara sugar over the top.

7. Bake 20-25 min, rotating halfway through until golden brown

8. Let cool, meanwhile whisk to combine glaze ingredients.

9. Drizzle glaze over cooled pastry
