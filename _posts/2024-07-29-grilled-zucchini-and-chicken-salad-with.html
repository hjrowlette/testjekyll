 This is a great summer recipe; I adapted it a little from the original to grill the zucchini rather than roast it in the oven. The goal with the zucchini is to get it nice and melty soft with a bit of char on it as well! Everything else including the dressing comes together super easily. I would store the dressing separately and combine with leftovers as you eat!


Roasted Zucchini Chicken Dill Salad

Ingredients

4 zucchini

1 lb chicken

olive oil

salt to taste

1 tbsp za’atar seasoning

1 large English cucumber diced

1 can chickpeas, rinsed and drained

1/2 cup chopped dill

1/4 cup chopped mint

1/4 cup chopped basil

1 block feta cheese

 Dressing

1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

1/4 cup apple cider vinegar

Juice 1 lemon

1 teaspoon dijon mustard

1 tbsp honey

1/2 tsp salt

 

Directions

  1. Halve the zucchini, then season with olive oil and salt to taste. Grill until soft/jammy. Cool slightly before chopping into pieces. Season chopped zucchini with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Season chicken with olive oil, salt, and za’atar. Grill until fully cooked through, then chop into pieces.
  3. In a medium bowl add all dressing ingredients. Use an immersion blender or a whisk to combine.
  4. Add all the remaining salad ingredients to a serving bowl. Top with the vinaigrette and serve!
