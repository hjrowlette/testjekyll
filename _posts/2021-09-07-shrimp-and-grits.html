Shrimp and Grits

Ingredients

1 cup dry grits 

4 cups water

2 cups shredded cheese (I used white cheddar)

2 tbsp butter

6 slices thick-cut bacon, chopped

1 lb of shrimp, shelled and deveined

3 cloves garlic

2 tsp lemon juice (fresh best)

cayenne

1 tsp fresh parsley, chopped

3 scallions, sliced


Directions

1. Boil 4 cups of heavily salted water, slowly add grits and cook per instructions until done (it was 20 minutes for me). Once grits have finished cooking, add butter and cheese, remove from heat and keep warm. If over thickening, you could add milk, half and half, or water to keep it loose.

2. In large saucepan cook bacon pieces over medium heat until browned and remove from pan. 

3. Leave no more than 4 tsp of bacon grease in the pan, and add in shrimp, garlic, cayenne, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Cook until shrimp are pink, then stir in chopped parsley

4. In bowls spoon the grits, top with shrimp, and sprinkle with scallions. Serve!
