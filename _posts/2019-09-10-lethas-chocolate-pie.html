Pie Filling Ingredients:


1 cup of sugar
3 heaping Tbsp Hersheys' Cocoa
3 heaping Tbsp Sugar
3 heaping Tbsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp salt
4 extra large egg yolks
12 oz can evaporated milk
1 Tbsp vanilla flavoring
6 Tbsp Shedds' spread

Directions:

Combine dry ingredients in a medium saucepan. Stir in egg yolks and mix well.  Stir in evaporated milk.  Cook on med high heat until mixture comes to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to med low.  Keep stirring an additional 2 minutes, mixture should thicken up.  Remove from heat, stir in vanilla flavoring and Shedds spread.

Pour mixture into baked pie shell.  Place plastic wrap on top of hot filling but not crust.  Refrigerate until cold.  Serve with whipped cream.

Pie crust: Makes 4 crusts


4 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 1/2 cups shortening
1 egg
1/2 cup plus 1 Tbsp ice water

Directions:

Sift dry ingredients.  Add shortening.  Mix with fork until mixture resembles cornmeal.  Stir egg and water together.  Add liquid to dry ingredients. Shape into 4 balls.  Chill 15 minutes.  Roll out on floured wax paper. Line pie plate pan with your pastry. Cut of excess crust and crimp edges. Prick with fork.  Line pan with foil extending over edges of crust. Add beans or pie weights. Bake at 425 degrees for 10-15 minutes. You can flatten these balls into disks, wrap in plastic, and freeze for next time.
