 This is a quick and easy grilled veggie recipe that I came across and loved! you could absolutely use it for different veggies, but the marinade and herbs are super tasty and paired really well with kofta or grilled pork. Enjoy!


Grilled Zucchini / Summer Veggies

Ingredients
2 lb zucchini (eggplant or summer squash would also be great)

2 tbsp EVOO

2 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp garlic powder

black pepper

1/2-1 tsp salt

4 tbsp lime juice

3 tsp fresh cilantro, chopped


Directions

1. Slice zucchini or other veggie lengthwise (quarter if very large)

2. Mix oil, pepper, salt, garlic powder, and oregano. Brush onto zucchini and let marinate 30-60 minutes

3. Place zucchini on the hot grill and grill until browned. Remove from heat

4. Mix lime juice and cilantro together, and pour over cooked zucchini. Serve and enjoy!
