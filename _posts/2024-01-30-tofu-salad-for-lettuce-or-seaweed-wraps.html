This is the simplest meal prep you'll find! Really easy to throw together and tweak flavoring to your preference, and goes great with some rice, avocado, and either dried seaweed or lettuce leaves for little wraps! I'm definitely having it for lunch all week, but would make a great summertime dinner too :)\

Tofu Salad for Lettuce or Seaweed Wraps

 
Ingredients:

1 block (~450g) very firm tofu
1 avocado (you'll need a second avocado for serving)
3 baby cucumbers, finely diced
3 scallions, finely sliced
1 ½ tbsp soy sauce, tamari, or teriyaki sauce

3 tbsp kewpie mayo (regular mayo would work)
3-4 tsp crispy chili oil

 

For serving

2-3 packs toasted seaweed or lettuce
1 avocado, sliced
white rice

Directions:
 1. Grate the tofu on a box grater. You won’t have to press hard for the tofu to grate, and it doesn’t have to be perfect.

2. Mix the tofu, 1 mashed avocado, cucumber, scallion, soy sauce, mayo, and chili oil. Taste and season with salt and adjust with any of the other sauces to your personal preference.

3.  Serve with rice and sliced avocado as well as dried seaweed or lettuce to make bite sized wraps. enjoy!
