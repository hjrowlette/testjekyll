I've been wanting to try cooking halloumi for a long time, and honestly just had a vision in my mind of what I wanted to eat it with! I pieced together a few bits of recipes and came up with the relatively quick, super delicious, and very light summer bowl. 


It's lemony, herby, nutty, and super well rounded. It would be a great mix in with some of the other Mediterranean recipes on here like the white bean hummus, kofta, or meal prep for a week if you wanted to make a true mix and match feast to satisfy anyone's tastes. I have a feeling I'm going to get addicted to just how easy and quick this whole thing was. Enjoy!

Grilled Halloumi and Zucchini on Herby Couscous with Tahini Dressing

Ingredients

Halloumi

Package of halloumi

Neutral oil

 

Zucchini

1-2 lb zucchini (eggplant or summer squash would also be great)

2 tbsp EVOO

2 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp garlic powder

black pepper

1/2-1 tsp salt

4 tbsp lime juice

3 tsp fresh cilantro, chopped

 

Herby Couscous

4 tbsp EVOO

2-3 garlic cloves, minced

2 cups chicken broth

4 tbsp fresh lemon juice

Zest from 1 lemon

2 cups couscous

4 tbsp chopped herbs (parsley, cilantro, basil)

Salt and pepper

 

Tahini Dressing

1/3 cup tahini

1 medium lemon, juiced

1-2 tbsp maple syrup or honey

1/2 tsp salt

1 clove garlic, minced/grated

Water (3-6 tbsp as needed)

 

Directions

Tahini Dressing

1.  Whisk all ingredients but water. Slowly add water until dressing is creamy and pourable. Set aside

 

Herby Couscous

1. Add EVOO and garlic to a pan over medium heat. Cook until garlic is fragrant and softened.

2. Remove from heat and add broth, lemon zest, and lemon juice.

3. Return to medium-high heat and bring to a boil. Pour in couscous, stir, and remove from heat. Immediately cover with lid, and let rest for 5 minutes off the heat.

4. Add herbs and fluff with fork. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and drizzle additional olive oil if desired.

 

Grilled Zucchini

1.  Slice zucchini lengthwise (quarter if very large).

2. Mix EVOO, pepper, salt, garlic powder, and oregano. Brush onto zucchini and let marinate 30-60 minutes.

3. Place zucchini on the hot grill and cook until browned. Remove from heat

4. Mix lime juice and cilantro and pour over cooked zucchini

 

For Grilled Halloumi

1. Remove halloumi from packaging and pat dry

2. Cut 1/2 inch thick slices. Lightly coat with neutral oil

3. Grill 2-3 minutes until charred to desired level.

 

Assemble Bowls

1. Lay a bed of the couscous. Top with thickly sliced zucchini and halloumi, then drizzle tahini dressing overtop. Top with additional herbs as desired. Could also add pickled onions, fresh cucumbers or tomatoes, or other toppings. Enjoy!
