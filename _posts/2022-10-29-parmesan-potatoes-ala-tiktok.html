 tiktok hit and its here to stay! super easy and quick potato recipe, very delicious and comes together quickly! I made it two weekends in a row if that attests to just how tasty this is. enjoy!


Parmesan Potatoes

Ingredients

1.5-2 lbs potatoes

one stick of butter

2 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped

2 tsp smoked paprika

2 tbsp spice blend (dealers choice)

~1 tsp salt (unless your spice blend contains salt, in which case adjust accordingly)

1 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese


Directions.

1. Preheat oven to 425 F 

2. Wash potatoes, cut in half lengthwise (if your potatoes are super thick cut in thirds rather than halves) and then score potatoes in a 3x3 without cutting all the way through the potato. 

3. Melt butter in the microwave, then mix in your herbs, spices, and parmesan to form a paste. Spread paste over the bottom of a 9x13 dish

4. Place potatoes (cut side down) on top of the paste. (lightly brush the tops of any potatoes you cut into thirds with the paste mixture) 

5. Bake for 35 minutes. LET COOL, then enjoy!
