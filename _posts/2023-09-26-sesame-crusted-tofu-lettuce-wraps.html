 I debated combining this with the last post, but decided the tofu alone is good enough to stand on its own, and this will be a quick post on one possible use for your amazingly delicious crusted tofu! It would be super easy to spin this based on what you've got around, so substitute herbs, spreads, or whatever other toppings you've got around to your hearts content!


Sesame Crusted Tofu Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

sesame crusted tofu

hummus

chili oil

sliced scallions

crispy onions

lettuce leaves


Directions

1. Lay lettuce leaves (2-3) on plate. Smear roughly 1 tbsp of tofu across the middle of each leaf. 

2. Slice tofu into strips, and place 2-3 strips on each lettuce leaf. Top with a drizzle of chili oil, sprinkle of scallions, and a few pieces of crispy onions. 

3. Enjoy!
