Heaven forbid this blog get boring, because we are shaking it up yet again! Don't be fooled by the photo, this lovely cake recipe is for the fido in your life :) it's super easy to make and freezes amazingly well! My only note is that you do want to freeze what you aren't using within a day or two of baking, because the first time I made it the cake went moldy very quickly. However once its frozen it thaws super easily and is ready to go on any occasion!





Dog Cake

Cake Ingredients

1 cup flour

1/2 tsp baking soda

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1/4 cup natural peanut butter (MAKE SURE THERE IS NO XYILTOL IN YOUR PEANUT BUTTER, IT ISN'T DOG SAFE)

1/2 cup applesauce, plain unsweetened (if you don't have apple
sauce you can just increase your pumpkin, or vice versa)

1/2 cup pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie mix, double check it!)

1 egg

Frosting Ingredients

1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt

1/4 cup peanut butter


Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350F

2. Combine flour and baking soda in a large bowl

3. In a separate bowl combine vegetable oil, peanut butter, applesauce, and pumpkin. Once mixed, add egg and stir to combine

4. Combine wet and dry ingredients and stir until combined.

4. Pour mixture into a lightly greased or parchment lined 8" round cake pan

6. Bake 20-30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Let cool

7. While baking, combine yogurt and peanut butter to make frosting. If not using immediately, store frosting in the fridge.

8. After cake is cooled, frost cake and decorate with carrots, cheese, a drizzle of honey, or anything else!
