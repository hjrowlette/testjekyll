Famous Tik Tok Salad! Super tasty, a very easy side to pack in the veggies. 

Dressing:

 1 c. basil leaves

1 c. spinach leaves

1/4c. cashews (or alternative nuts)

Juice of two lemons

1 shallot, halved

2 cloves garlic, crushed

1/3c. nutritional yeast**

3 Tbsp. rice wine vinegar

1/4 c. olive oil

1 tsp salt


**I subbed nutritional yeast in favor of an avocado

Combine all these ingredients in blender, blend until smooth. Taste and Adjust

OPTIONAL: Add avocado!

Salad:

1 small head green cabbage, diced small

1 cucumber, diced small (or 3 Persian cucumbers)

1 bunch green onions, diced small

OPTIONAL: Add jalapeno!

Place all diced vegetables in bowl and toss with dressing. Taste and adjust for salt and vinegar.

Enjoy with chips!
