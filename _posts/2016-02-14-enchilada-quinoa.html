So I have officially broken in my crockpot with a pretty fantastic recipe that I want to share. I have never cooked quinoa before, and I have to say if it tastes this good every time I may be officially converted. Mom and Brittany this recipe tastes a lot like a healthier version of Texas Beef. It uses ground turkey which I am a fan of for taste and price, and it has your basic tex-mex ingredients!



Basically you put everything but the cheese and cilantro into the slow cooker and let it sit for a few hours and that is about it-couldn't get much simpler (: I've included some pictures from the website as well as some of from when I made it this weekend!! Happy cooking, and a lovely valentines day to you fantastic ladies <3
