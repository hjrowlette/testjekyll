This recipe can be used as a variation on a cold chicken salad, and pairs really well as a summery filling for arepas (see separate recipe post on how to make them!)

There isn't much to go into for this recipe, its really quick and easy and comes together all in one bowl-enjoy!



Ingredients
1 pound shredded poached chicken (can use any available shredded chicken really)
2 Haas avocados, peeled, pitted, roughly mashed
2-3 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
½ red bell pepper, seeds and ribs removed, diced
3 tablespoons finely chopped white onion
3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
½ jalapeño pepper, seeds and ribs removed, finely chopped

2 teaspoons finely minced garlic (2 medium cloves)
½ teaspoon salt or more, to taste
Fresh ground black pepper

Directions
In a medium bowl mix the mashed avocado with mayonnaise and lime juice. Add the rest of the ingredients: chicken, bell pepper, onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and garlic. Season to taste with salt
