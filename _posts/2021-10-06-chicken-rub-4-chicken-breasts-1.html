  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon of Paprika

Grill it, pan it, bake it until cooked.

Paprika Spice Blend

  • 4 Parts Smoked Paprika
  • 3 Parts Garlic Powder
  • 1 Part Onion Powder
  • 3 Part Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Dash Cayenne Pepper
  • (Optional) 3 Part Brown Sugar
  • Put in an appropriate amount of salt for your meat :P

Bake it up baby 425 for 20ish minutes 

Did 4 Chicken Thighs with 1 Part being 1/2 tsp as reference and it was a bit over seasoned.
