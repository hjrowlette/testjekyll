Potato Salad

Ingredients

1/2 cup cooked bacon, chopped

1/4 cup milk

1/4 cup sour cream

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1 1/2 tsp ranch salad dressing mix

2 1/2 lbs red potatoes (if grilling, in 1/4 in thick slices) (if oven, 1/4 in cubes)

2 stalks celery, chopped

Salt and pepper

 

Directions

1. For dressing, in a medium bowl combine the milk, sour cream, mayo, salad dressing mix, and celery. Cover and chill until ready to serve.

2. Grill potatoes over medium heat for 20-25 minutes until fork tender. Alternatively, mix with oil, salt, and pepper and bake at 400 until fork tender, 30ish minutes.

3. In a large bowl combine the potatoes and the bacon. Fold in the dressing until combined. Season with salt and pepper. Chill 1-2 hours before serving
