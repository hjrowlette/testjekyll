continuing the summer 2023 obsession with halloumi! this is a quick and easy summer dinner that would be awesome to take advantage of garden or farmers market tomatoes--if you don't have hot honey you can mix regular honey with a cayenne pepper until its the right heat level for you--no need to purchase a separate honey product! 



Marinated Tomato and Grilled Halloumi Sandwich

Ingredients

Baguette or other bread

Heirloom tomatoes

Shallot

Oregano

Vinegar (I used white wine vinegar, champagne would also be good)

EVOO

Halloumi

Hot honey

 

Directions

1. finely chop shallots. Mix with oregano, vinegar, and EVOO in a medium bowl

2. Thickly slice tomatoes, and season salt and pepper. Add to bowl with shallot vinaigrette.

3. Slice and fry halloumi until golden brown. Slice and toast baguette.

4. Spoon vinaigrette onto baguette, then layer halloumi and tomato. Drizzle with hot honey.

5. Eat immediately, preferably with a side of zucchini or a salad!
