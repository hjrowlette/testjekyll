Spiced Lentil and Chickpea Salad with Halloumi

 Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil

1 red onion, halved and sliced thinly

About 8oz or 1 cup cherry vine or other small tomatoes

2 tsp harissa (if using dry rather than paste, add a little EVOO and water)

250g cooked French or beluga lentils (125g dry lentils)

Â½ lemon, juiced

1 block halloumi, cut into Â¼ inch slices (could sub feta or goat cheese instead, just don't fry it)

1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed

a small bunch mint, roughly torn

a small bunch flat-leaf parsley, roughly torn

 

Dressing

1 tbsp tahini

3 tbsp natural yogurt

2-3 tsp harissa (if using dry rather than paste, add a little EVOO and water)

1 tsp honey

Â½ lemon, juiced

Â½ a clove garlic, minced

 

Directions

1. Whisk together all the dressing ingredients; slowly add enough water to make a drizzle-able consistency.

2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and cook the onion until soft. Stir in the tomatoes and cook until they are just starting to break down and burst open.

3. Stir in the harissa, lentils and lemon juice, cook for a minute then season well and cool.

4. In a separate dry, nonstick pan (or on the grill), fry the halloumi slices in batches until golden on both sides.

5. Add the chickpeas and 1/2 the herbs into the lentil mixture.

6. Put on a platter or divide between plates and top with the dressing, the halloumi and the remaining herbs.
