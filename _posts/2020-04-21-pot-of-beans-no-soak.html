In all reality, these beans are incredible. They're so easy, so delicious, and I'm absolutely going to be using this recipe again and again. The beauty is not just its speed, but it's versatility. Use black beans, northern beans, pinto beans--it doesn't matter. Don't have chipotle in adobo? Substitute a fresh jalapeno. Missing rosemary? Toss in a basil or oregano!

Keep your eyes peeled for a few recipes to come with ways to use these tasty beans!




No-Soak Pot of Beans

 Ingredients
1 lb dry beans (I used black beans)
roughly 10 cups water (enough to cover beans by an inch or inch and a half)
rosemary sprig



1 bay leaf
1 chipotle in adobo
1 glug EVOO
roughly 2 tsp salt
roughly 1 tsp chicken bouillon
1 medium onion, chopped
3 clove garlic, minced

other ideas: miso paste, smoked meat, dried chilis



Directions
1.  Preheat oven to 325â„‰. Rinse beans and put in oven-save pot (dutch oven is great)

2. Cover beans by 1 to 1.5 inches of water. Add all other ingredients you're using to the pot

3. Bring pot to a simmer on the stove, cover, then place on center rack of the oven. Cook in the oven for around 75 minutes. (If you want to do this on the stove, just keep it at a bare simmer for the time)
